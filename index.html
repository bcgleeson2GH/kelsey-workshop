Workshop Agenda

What We'll Do Together

Six fast-moving conversations, each with a short working exercise. You'll leave with clarity and momentum.

1

Freedom from the Business

Map your A/B/C players and choose one immediate action to upgrade your team. Start moving toward a business that doesn't require you to be everywhere.

2

Culture & Core

Clarify your real values—not just the ones on the wall—and identify one behavior change that would meaningfully improve your results.

3

Strategic Clarity

Capture your 3-year "freedom vision" on one page and define the single most important move you can make in the next 12 months.

4

Stand Out in the Market

Sharpen your core customer profile and draft a brand promise that makes choosing your company an easier decision for the right people.

5

Value Drivers

Rate your business on key value drivers—team strength, profitability, customer mix—and pinpoint where improvement would have the biggest payoff.

6

Profit, Cash & Simple Valuation Lens

Use a simple exercise to see how a small improvement in profit could add meaningful long-term wealth—without a technical finance lesson.

Why This Matters Now

Even if you're not thinking about selling…

You might not be planning to exit any time soon—and that's exactly the point.

The same decisions that:

  • Give you more freedom from the business
  • Help you attract and keep better people
  • Improve margins and cash flow
  • Make growth more predictable

…are the decisions that quietly build the long-term value of your company and your personal options in the future.

This workshop is about building that kind of business—one that works for you now and sets you up for whatever you decide later.

The businesses that scale—and sell on their own terms—aren't the ones that got lucky.

They made intentional, compounding decisions about their people, their strategy, and their systems. This workshop is your chance to make a few of those decisions—with clarity, and with other business owners in the room.
